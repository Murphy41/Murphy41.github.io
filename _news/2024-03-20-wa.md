I traveled to a remote mine site in **Western Australia** (a ~2-hour flight from Perth) to conduct a field investigation for my PhD thesis on **mining robotics**.

Gaining firsthand experience of the unstructured terrain and harsh conditions is essential for developing robust autonomous systems. The vast, rugged landscape serves as the ultimate testing ground for our research.
